What is this?

Freicoin is a cryptocurrency launched in 2012 — one of the oldest chains alive — built to test the economics of Silvio Gesell (1862–1930): money should age, like the goods it buys. Every unspent coin loses ≈3.3% per year to demurrage, and exactly that much is re-minted to the miners who secure the chain — forever, with no halvings.

The consequences are unlike any other cryptocurrency: the supply provably converges to 100,000,000 FRC and stays there; security has a permanent budget that never depends on fees; and hoarding is possible but expensive — this is money designed to move, not to be a lottery ticket. Saving is what capital and Bitcoin are for; Freicoin is a medium of exchange.

Technically it is a cousin of Bitcoin: SHA256d proof-of-work merge-mined with Bitcoin (your Bitcoin hashrate can secure Freicoin at no extra cost), MAST script trees, and a codebase kept current with upstream — it builds and fully validates on Bitcoin Core v31.1 (details).

Run a node

Stock freicoind v28.1 bootstraps out of the box with -seednode=seed.testtty.ru. A full sync is under an hour — the chain is light (~800 MB).

Honesty first: this is a small network with a thin market. Don't bring money you can't lose — bring curiosity. What you get in exchange is the only place on earth where Gesell's century-old idea runs, measurably, block by block.

Community infrastructure: wallet · explorer · mining · seed.testtty.ru · stratum.testtty.ru — run by volunteers, mirrors welcome.
