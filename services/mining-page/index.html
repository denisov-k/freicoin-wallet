⛏ Mine Freicoin

Freicoin (2012) is a SHA256d chain with demurrage economics and a perpetual block reward — 95.37 FRC every ~15 minutes, forever, no halvings. The whole network currently runs on a few TH/s, so even a single small miner earns a real share. Payouts are trustless: blocks pay your address directly in the coinbase.

algorithmSHA256d (any Bitcoin ASIC: Antminer, Bitaxe, …)
block reward95.36743164 FRC, perpetual
blocks per day~96 (15-minute spacing)
payoutdirectly to your address in the coinbase — non-custodial, no registration

1. Get an address

Create a wallet at wallet.testtty.ru (12 words, keys stay on your device) and copy your fc1q… receive address.

2. Point your miner

URL:      stratum+tcp://stratum.testtty.ru:9638
Worker:   fc1q…your-address…
Password: x

That's it. This is solo mining: shares don't accumulate — when your miner finds a block, the block itself pays your address 95.37 FRC. With today's network size that is a lottery with unusually good odds (a 1 TH/s Bitaxe is a double-digit percentage of the whole network), but it is still a lottery: expect nothing for days, then a whole block.

Mined coins mature for 100 blocks (~a day) before they are spendable; the wallet shows them as pending until then.

Prefer your own node? (recommended for serious mining)

The endpoint above is a stock freicoind — you can run the same thing and depend on no one:

# freicoin.conf
server=1
stratum=1
stratumbind=127.0.0.1        # or your LAN
stratumallowip=192.168.0.0/16
# stratum port defaults to 9638 on mainnet

Node: tradecraft releases (Freicoin v28.1). Bootstrap works out of the box with -seednode=seed.testtty.ru.

Pool operator? Real merged mining

Freicoin is merge-mined against Bitcoin: your pool can collect the FRC emission with the hashrate you already have, at no extra ASIC time. freicoind ships the coordination protocol (mining.aux.subscribe / notify / submit); your side places a 36-byte commitment in your Bitcoin block template. Includes block-withholding protection. See doc/auxpow.md — and reach out on the tracker, we'll help you integrate.

⚠ Honesty note: at current prices the whole network's emission is worth a few tens of dollars a day. Mine it because it's a living piece of 2012 crypto history with unique economics — not for the money. The stratum.testtty.ru endpoint is community infrastructure, best-effort.
